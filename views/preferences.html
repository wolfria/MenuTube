Controls

Use media keys and shortcuts on keyboard to manage video/audio

- play or Pause video

- play next video in a playlist

- play previous video in a playlist

"Cmd(⌘) + Ctrl(^) + Right Arrow(→)" - jump 5 sec forward

"Cmd(⌘) + Ctrl(^) + Left Arrow(←)" - jump 5 sec backward

"Cmd(⌘) + Ctrl(^) + L" - jump 15 sec forward

"Cmd(⌘) + Ctrl(^) + J" - jump 15 sec backward

"Cmd(⌘) + Ctrl(^) + Y" - toggle window
